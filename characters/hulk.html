The Incredible Hulk

Incredible Hulk, American comic strip character created for Marvel Comics by writer Stan Lee and artist Jack Kirby. The towering muscle-bound antihero debuted in the bimonthly series The Incredible Hulk in May 1962.

Incredible superhuman strength, durability, and healing factor. Becomes more powerful as anger increases. As Bruce Banner, possesses a genius-level intellect & is an expert in multiple scientific fields, particularly the studies of physics & radiation.
