Deadpool

Deadpool is a fictional character appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. Created by writer Fabian Nicieza and artist/writer Rob Liefeld, the character first appeared in The New Mutants #98 (cover-dated February 1991).

Super Powers

Deadpool's powers include a long list of abilities up to and including Regeneration, Extended longevity, Skilled marksman, swordsman, and hand-to-hand combatant, Using devices granting teleportation, and holographic disguise, Superhuman strength, durability, and agility
